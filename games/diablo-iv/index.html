📖 About Seasons
Diablo IV is an action RPG developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment. It returns to the dark, gothic roots of the series, featuring a vast shared open world of Sanctuary. Diablo IV operates on a quarterly seasonal model, with each season introducing new storylines, unique mechanics, class balance updates, and new Battle Pass rewards.

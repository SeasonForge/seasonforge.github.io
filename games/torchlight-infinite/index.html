📖 About Seasons
Torchlight: Infinite is a loot-based action RPG developed by XD. Operating as a free-to-play game, it focuses on high-speed combat, build freedom, and diverse hero customization. Seasons run approximately every 3 months, introducing new playable heroes, exclusive league mechanics, crafting updates, and fresh endgame challenges.
